    About US

    In a world teeming with challenges and adversities, where the vulnerable often struggle for a glimmer of hope and a healing touch, there arises a beacon of compassion, a stronghold of altruism - the Omvati Devi Welfare Foundation. Born from the vision of benevolence and nurtured by unwavering determination, this non-governmental organization (NGO) has dedicated itself to uplifting the lives of the less fortunate through a diverse range of initiatives. With a primary focus on medical assistance, the Omvati Devi Welfare Foundation has become a vital source of comfort, hope, and healing for countless individuals.

    Long Term Planning

    Develop sustainable models to ensure our initiatives continue to make a lasting impact. Research and Innovation: Invest in research to find innovative solutions for healthcare, education, and animal welfare. Advocacy: Advocate for policies that improve the lives of the underprivileged and animals, working in collaboration with government bodies and other NGOs.

    Medical Assistance

    Health Camps: Conduct regular health camps, offering free medical check-ups, consultations, and medications to the underprivileged in both urban and rural areas. Specialized Care: Collaborate with healthcare professionals to provide specialized treatments and surgeries to those in need. Medicine Distribution: Facilitate access to essential medications and medical supplies, ensuring that no one is left without necessary treatment.

    Animal Shelters

    Establish and maintain shelters for injured and abandoned animals, promoting compassion and care for all living beings. Awareness Programs: Conduct awareness campaigns on animal rights and responsible pet ownership. Spay/Neuter Drives: Organize spaying and neutering campaigns to control the population of stray animals humanely.

    Donation Drives

    Mobilize resources and support from individuals, organizations, and corporations to fund our initiatives. Community Events: Host events that unite communities in a spirit of compassion and service. Volunteer Opportunities: Encourage active participation from volunteers who share our vision and values.

    Expanding Our Horizon

    While our foundation has its roots firmly embedded in medical assistance, we believe in the power of comprehensive care. To this end, we are now extending our reach to education and animal welfare. Our expansion signifies our commitment to holistic well-being and empowerment. Just as healing is not confined to the body alone, so too, our vision is not limited by the walls of any one sector. We are venturing into education to nourish minds, and into animal welfare to nurture compassion in the hearts of the community.

    Our Mission

    At the core of the Omvati Devi Welfare Foundation lies a mission to be the hand that reaches out in times of need, the voice that speaks for the voiceless, and the heart that cares without boundaries. Our mission is to provide comprehensive healthcare support to those in dire straits and extend our compassion to the broader community by fostering education and animal welfare. We strive to empower individuals, families, and communities, enabling them to lead healthier, more fulfilling lives.

    Objectives

    The Omvati Devi Welfare Foundation is more than an NGO; it is a movement that recognizes the interconnectedness of all living beings. We invite you to join us in our journey of compassion, to make a difference, and to be part of the change we wish to see in the world. Together, we can foster hope, healing, and transformation, one life at a time.
